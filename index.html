CA Coaching
Done Differently

Pinnacle Learning Hub is an offline CA coaching institute in Indore built on one principle — every student deserves structured, attentive, and exam-focused guidance through every stage of the CA journey.

ICAI Module-Mapped Teaching
Every class is directly aligned with ICAI study materials. You study exactly what the Institute expects — no detours, no guesswork.
Small Batches, Individual Attention
Deliberately small batch sizes ensure every student receives focused guidance, personalised doubt resolution, and direct faculty access.
Extended Study Hours at the Institute

Study Material

Notes Built for
How You Learn

Every student absorbs information differently. Pinnacle provides structured study material in multiple formats — so your preparation never stops, wherever you are.

Printed Structured Notes
Professionally designed printed notes for every subject — chapter-wise, exam-weightage prioritised, and ICAI module-mapped. Your primary revision companion right through to exam day.
Audio Study Notes
Study on the move — audio notes covering key concepts, definitions, case law summaries, and revision points. Ideal for commuting or reinforcing what you studied in class.
ICAI Module Integration
Our notes enhance the ICAI module, never replace it. Every note references the relevant module section so students understand the direct connection to official ICAI study material.
Past Paper Practice Sets
Practice sets drawn from ICAI past examination papers — subject-wise, topic-wise, and exam-cycle aligned. Understand the pattern, marking scheme, and examiner expectations before the real paper.
